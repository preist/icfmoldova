Who We Are

Simply put we are friends and followers of Jesus. As we follow Him, we discover how our own stories intersect with God's greater story.

Through our time together, we hope you will discover how deeply Jesus cares about you and your story. We hope that you will experience the joy that comes from surrendering your life to Jesus Christ and walking with Him.
